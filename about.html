About

Harmony Essence Creations was born from a journey across the world. After five enriching years as a Flight Attendant, exploring diverse cultures and landscapes, I discovered the profound impact of scents on our senses and well-being. Inspired by the fragrances encountered during my travels, particularly in Asian countries celebrated for their aromatic traditions, I felt compelled to share these sensory experiences with others.

Our candles are crafted with care, using only natural ingredients, 100 % Pure Soy Wax. Thus ensuring a clean burn that is vegan-friendly, sustainable, and biodegradable. We select high-quality fragrances that are both phthalate-free and paraben-free, evoking memories of distant lands and enriching experiences. At Harmony Essence Creations, our aim is simple: to bring a piece of the world into your home through our candles and to inspire moments of tranquillity and reflection. Join us in illuminating your space with the gentle glow of our handcrafted soy candles, each one a reminder of the beauty found in simplicity and the harmony between nature and human creativity.
